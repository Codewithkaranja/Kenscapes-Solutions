Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

Our Contact Process

Here's what happens after you reach out to us

1

Initial Contact

You reach out via phone, WhatsApp, email, or our contact form. We respond within 24 hours to schedule a consultation.

2

Site Visit & Consultation

We visit your property (free within Nairobi) to assess the space, discuss your vision, and understand your requirements.

3

Design & Proposal

We create a custom design and detailed proposal including timeline, materials, and cost breakdown.

4

Project Approval

We review the proposal with you, make any adjustments, and once approved, schedule the project start date.

5

Project Execution

Our skilled team begins work according to the agreed timeline, with regular updates throughout the process.
