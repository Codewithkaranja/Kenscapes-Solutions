Our Journey

From passion to professional landscaping excellence

Kenscapes Solutions was founded with a passion for landscaping and a deep appreciation for nature. The company was created to help homeowners, businesses, and institutions transform their outdoor spaces into green, functional, and visually appealing environments.

What began as a personal passion for creating beautiful outdoor spaces has grown into a professional landscaping company known for quality workmanship, creative designs, and sustainable practices. Our journey reflects our commitment to bringing nature closer to people's lives while enhancing property value and environmental balance.

Over the years, we've transformed hundreds of outdoor spaces across Nairobi and surrounding areas, building a reputation for reliability, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction. Each project has taught us something new, helping us refine our approach and expand our expertise.
